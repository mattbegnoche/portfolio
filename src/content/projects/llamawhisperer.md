A WisprFlow alternative. Press a hotkey, speak, press it again: your words are transcribed by Whisper, cleaned up by a local LLM via Ollama (punctuation, capitalization, filler-word removal), and pasted straight into whatever app has focus.

Everything runs on-device — no cloud APIs, no subscriptions, works fully offline.
